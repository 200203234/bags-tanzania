Our Story

From humble beginnings to Tanzania's premier bag retailer

BAGS Tanzania was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, stylish bags that combine functionality, durability, and fashion. What started as a small family business has grown into one of Tanzania's most trusted bag retailers.

Our journey began when our founder, Michael Robert, noticed a gap in the market for quality bags that could withstand Tanzania's diverse climate while still looking stylish. He started by importing a small selection of travel bags and quickly gained a reputation for quality and reliability.

Today, we offer a wide range of bags for every occasion - from rugged travel backpacks to elegant professional briefcases. Our collections are carefully curated to meet the diverse needs of our customers, from students to professionals and travelers.

At BAGS TANZANIA, we believe that a good bag is more than just an accessory - it's a companion for life's journeys. That's why we're committed to providing products that not only look great but are built to last.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our company's history

2010
BAGS TANZANIA was founded with a small store in Dar es Salaam, focusing on travel bags.
2013
Expanded our product line to include school bags and launched our first website.
2016
Opened two additional stores in Arusha and Mwanza, growing our presence across Tanzania.
2019
Launched our premium collection and introduced eco-friendly bag options.
2022
Celebrated serving over 50,000 customers and expanded our online store capabilities.
2023
Introduced custom bag design services and partnered with local artisans.
